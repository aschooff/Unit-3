Demographic Composition of Janesville Wards

This map and coordinated visualization shows the demographic composition of each ward in the City of Janesville in the year 2020. The data used in this analysis is sourced from GeoData@Wisconsin, an online geoportal that provides access to spatial data for the state of Wisconsin. The data was collected by state agencies, and can be found here. The data shows the percentage of residents in each ward that identify as either White, Black, Hispanic, Asian, American Indian, Pacific Islander, or Other. Use the dropdown menu above the map to select a demographic category of interest, and use the mouse to hover over a bar on the chart or a ward on the map to see it's percentage of people that identify under the selected demographic.
